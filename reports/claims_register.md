## The four reviewer questions

1. **Better than OCV-inverse + Coulomb?** Marginally OOD (LSTM), clearly on MaxE, clearly with TCN; *no* in-distribution. (Phase 2)
2. **Better than vanilla + post-hoc clamp?** Yes, decisively — training-through-constraint is the real contribution. (Phase 2)
3. **Is −20 °C failure anchor-driven?** Yes — ~96 % of error; recursive inference removes most of it without retraining. (Phase 4, 6)
4. **Is the vanilla baseline fair?** Implementation correct; behavior explained; comparison must add ε-dead-band and last-step framing. (Phase 3)
